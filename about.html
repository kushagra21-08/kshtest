The Heart of Our Family Dairy

At KSHEERAALAY, we believe that the best dairy comes from a place of trust and care. Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the pure, artisanal quality of Gir cow milk directly to your home.

Our Methods

Gir Cow Welfare

We prioritize the health and happiness of our herd. Our Gir cows are raised in a natural environment with ample space and premium nutrition.

Traditional Bilona Process

Our Bilona ghee is made using time-honored techniques. We use only the finest raw milk and a natural stone press to extract the purest essence.

Farm to Home Purity

We maintain a direct link between our farm and your kitchen. No chemicals, no preservatives — just the purest milk and dairy products from our doorstep.
