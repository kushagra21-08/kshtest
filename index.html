The Essence of Bilona Ghee

Bilona Ghee is crafted using the traditional Vedic method, where curd is hand-churned to extract butter before being slowly simmered into pure ghee. This time-honored process preserves its rich aroma, natural nutrients, and authentic flavor. Made from ethically sourced milk, Bilona Ghee embodies purity, wellness, and the heritage of traditional Indian craftsmanship in every spoonful..
